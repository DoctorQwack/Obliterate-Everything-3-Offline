Offline Gameplay Guide

Welcome to the customized offline build of OE3! All network requirements have been replaced with a simulated client-side server.

  • Profiles: Create profiles or login with any username on the account screen. They will save locally.
  • Persistence: Your credits, inventory, armory, and campaign maps are saved directly in your browser's Local Storage. Clearing your browser data will wipe your saves.
  • Offline Features: Play the Single Player campaign, unlock advanced tech in the Store, upgrade weapons, and customize your ship loadouts in the Hangar.

Key Bindings

  • 1 - 7 : Build units in slots 1 to 7
  • Q, W, E : Advanced build options
  • A, S : Secondary utilities
  • Z, X : Structural abilities
  • Space : Pause / Resume match
  • Click & Drag : Pan the camera around the map grid
