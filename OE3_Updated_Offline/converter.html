How to Find Your Kongregate User ID

                
            

            
            

                
Since Kongregate save data is stored under your numeric User ID (e.g., kong12345678) instead of your username, you may need your numeric ID to find or verify your save. Here are the easiest ways to find it:

                
                

                    

                        1
                        Search by Username in the Converter
                    

                    
Simply type your readable Kongregate username (e.g., CristianoP5) into the search bar. This tool parses both IDs and callsigns from the database, allowing you to find your account immediately without needing the numeric ID!

                


                

                    

                        2
                        Copy Your Avatar Image Link
                    

                    
Go to your profile page on Kongregate. Right-click your avatar image and choose "Copy Image Link" (or open image in new tab). The link looks like: 

                    https://cdn1.kongcdn.com/user_avatars/0012/3456/avatar.png
                    Combine the numeric folders and remove leading zeros to get your User ID: 00123456 → 123456.

                


                

                    

                        3
                        View Profile Page Source
                    

                    
While logged into Kongregate, open your profile page. Right-click anywhere and select "View Page Source" (or press Ctrl + U). Search (Ctrl + F) for "kong_user_data". You will see your ID: "id":123456.
